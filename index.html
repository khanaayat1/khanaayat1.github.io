§01 About the researcher

Where mind, behavior & machine meet

I'm a second-year undergraduate Psychology major at Women's College Srinagar, passionate about understanding human behavior through the combined lens of science and technology.

My curiosity sits at the crossing point of cognitive psychology, behavioral neuroscience, and mental health — and increasingly, of artificial intelligence. I'm drawn to research and data analysis as tools for turning observation into insight.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy picking up new technologies and finding ways to combine psychology with AI to solve real-world problems — from modeling decision-making to exploring how machines might one day help us understand our own minds.
